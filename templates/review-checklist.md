# Review Checklist

Use this checklist before moving content from `ready-for-review` to `approved`.

- [ ] Content aligns with AI Constitution doctrine intent (without rewriting source doctrine).
- [ ] Claims and references were verified by a human reviewer.
- [ ] Article structure follows `templates/article-template.md`.
- [ ] WordPress metadata follows `templates/wordpress-frontmatter-template.md`.
- [ ] Target WordPress status is explicitly `draft`.
- [ ] No secrets, credentials, tokens, or sensitive data are present.
- [ ] Reviewer identity and decision timestamp are recorded.
