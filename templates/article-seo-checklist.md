# Article SEO Checklist

Complete this checklist before promotion to `content/approved/`.

## Core SEO Metadata
- [ ] `seo_title` present and in target range (50-60 chars)
- [ ] `seo_description` present and in target range (140-160 chars)
- [ ] `seo_keywords` provided (3-8 focused terms)
- [ ] `excerpt` provided and aligned to article intent
- [ ] Primary keyword appears naturally in title and opening section

## URL & Canonical
- [ ] `slug` is concise and human-readable
- [ ] `canonical_url` is set to a real URL or `TBD_CANONICAL_URL`
- [ ] Canonical placeholder use is explicitly acknowledged by reviewer if unresolved

## Taxonomy & Discovery
- [ ] `wp_categories` uses approved category slugs
- [ ] `wp_tags` uses approved tag slugs
- [ ] Tags are relevant and non-duplicative

## Quality and Governance
- [ ] Headings are structured for readability
- [ ] Claims are supportable and not misleading
- [ ] No prohibited/autonomous publish instructions are included
- [ ] Final intended WordPress status is `draft` only
